Log observed along the path of the borehole. <br/>Those logs are the result of an interpretation by a human being (driller, geologist) where  specific features (lithostratigraphicunit, contacts, ... ) are recognized.<br/>They differ from observation of natural parameters acquired by sensing devices (ex : geophysics and imagery). Those are considered in this model through the Monitoring Facility class and associated Measurements (and potential OutOfBandResult).<br/>
