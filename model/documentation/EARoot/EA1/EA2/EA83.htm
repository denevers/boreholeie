The ManagementArea represents an area of ground identified for management purposes. The area can be delineated by human factors such as policy or regulation concerns, as well as by domain concerns (ex : hydrogeological, hydrological). <br/>The spatial boundaries of a management area do not necessarily align exactly with associated georesource feature boundaries. ManagementArea has the potential to provide a pattern for a more generic OGC 'trans-domain' feature management class. ManagementArea is equivalent to InspireAM:ManagementRestrictionOrRegulationZone (adapted from GWML2)<br/>
